HISTORY OF THE FONT

Similar to its serif counterpart, this typeface draws inspiration from the lettering featured on a Chinese banknote from 1918. However, even though its origins trace back more than a hundred years, it also evokes the characteristics of mid-20th century sans serifs such as Microgramma and Eurostile, along with the squared gothic styles of the 1970s, including Neographik and Serpentine.
