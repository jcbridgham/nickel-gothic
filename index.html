HISTORY OF THE FONT

Nickel Gothic is a sans-serif typeface designed by James Montalbano and released through Terminal Design in 2018. The design was inspired by the typefaces of the 19th century and features a large x-height and short descenders. The family is available in four weights with matching italics.
